Sauce Bordelaise

The chicken tikka cames from india but it became a true classic in britain. It is a mild curry.

Ingredients

  • 2 onions
  • 1 TableSpoon ginger
  • 1 thai chili
  • 1 TableSpoon cumin
  • 1 TableSpoon coriander seeds
  • 1 TableSpoon fenugreck
  • 2 TableSpoon tomato paste
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 500ml chicken stock
  • 500mil cream

Steps

  1. confit all the veggies with the spice in pan in 50ml of canola oil or ghee until the veggie are brown.
  2. add the tomato paste a cook it for 5 to 10 minutes for in to become sweet.
  3. after that put the crushed tomatoes and cook them until the tomatoes start to confit and become a darker red.
  4. add your chicken with the chicken stock and the cream and braise it until the chicken can be pierce with a knifes very easily.
  5. serve it with rice and naans with a yoghurt with cucumbre a cilantro crush into it.

hope you enjoy.
